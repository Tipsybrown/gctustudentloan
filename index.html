WELCOME TO GHANA COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY UNIVERSITY STUDENT LOAN

GHANA COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY UNIVERSITY is giving hope to all it's Ghanaian deprived students who cannot afford to pay their tution fees to be able to further their education by giving loans to such students.After the loan has been paid into your account GHANA COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY UNIVERSITY offers you the best flexibility of paying back the loan at anytime you want to when you are a student and even if you have graduated GHANA COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY UNIVERSITY allows you to pay the loan 2years after graduation and in installments. To apply, you first need to create an account.

REQUIREMENTS FOR LOAN APPLICATION

To be able to qualify for a Ghana Communication Technology University Student Loan, students must meet the following requirements;

1.Persons must be a student of the Ghana Communications Technology University

2.Persons must have a student ID

3.Persons must be a Ghanaian and must have a valid Ghana card

.Persons must have records of his/her academic performance

5.Persons must have an active bank account

IF YOU DO NOT HAVE AN ACCOUNT WITH GCTU LOAN, CLICK ON THE APPLY BUTTON TO CREATE AN ACCOUNT AND APPLY FOR A LOAN

IF YOU ALREADY HAVE AN ACCOUNT CLICK ON THE LOGIN BUTTON TO TAKE YOU TO OUR LOGIN AND CHECK YOUR LOAN STATUS
